                 Privacy Policy for Kitty

Effective Date: 1st July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kitty ("the Bot") collects, 
uses, stores, and protects data from its users and Discord
servers. By adding the Bot to your server or interacting with
it, you agree to the data practices described below.

1. Data We Collect
We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide
and maintain the Bot's services. This may include:
1.1 Discord Identifiers: User IDs, Server (Guild) IDs, and
Channel IDs to route commands and save server-specific
configurations.
1.2 User Content: Specific command arguments or settings you
input to customize the Bot.
1.3 Premium Billing Data: If you purchase a subscription, our
third-party payment processors (like Stripe, PayPal, or Discord)
collect your payment information. We do not see or store your
credit card details.
1.4 Technical Logs: Temporary logs of bot errors and command
usage to help us fix bugs and prevent abuse.

2. How We Use Your Data
We use the collected information strictly to:
2.1 Run the Bot and provide its free and paid premium features.
2.2 Save your custom settings, preferences, and premium status.
2.3 Debug technical issues, improve performance, and prevent
spam or malicious activity.

3. Data Storage and Retention
3.1 Storage Security: Your configuration data is stored securely
using industry-standard databases and access controls.
3.2 Retention Period: We keep your data only as long as
necessary to provide the service.
3.3 Data Deletion: If you remove (kick) the Bot from your
server, your server's configuration data is automatically
deleted or marked for deletion within [e.g., 30 days].

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We never sell or trade your personal data to third parties. We
only share data with trusted services essential to operating
the Bot:
4.1 Discord Inc.: To process bot interactions via the official
Discord API.
4.2 Payment Processors: Stripe / PayPal / Discord App
Subscriptions to securely handle premium transactions.

You have full control over your data. You have the right to
